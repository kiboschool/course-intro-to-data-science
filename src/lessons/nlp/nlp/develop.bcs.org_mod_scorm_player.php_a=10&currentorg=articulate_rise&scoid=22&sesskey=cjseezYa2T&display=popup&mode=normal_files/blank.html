AI and the 4th Industrial Revolution

Innovations in digital technology are continuously influencing society, changing how we live our lives and how businesses operate. With further developments in Artificial Intelligence, the lines between human and machine are beginning to blur as we enter an age in which Artificial Intelligence has now becoming embedded within our homes (e.g. SMART devices), within the products and services we use, and within the technology in our workplaces.
